About me

Hi, I’m Aditya Anil — a programmer, writer, and lifelong learner with a passion for AI, technology, and science.

My story started at the age of 16 with Chegg, which launched my journey into technical writing. For over five years, I’ve written for outlets like Towards AI, Decoder, DataDrivenInvestor, and AI in Plain English — breaking down complex ideas in AI and computer science into clear, engaging articles.

While writing, I drifted toward programming — the more I explained systems, the more I wanted to build them. From HTML experiments to full-stack projects, this curiosity pulled me into open source and the inner workings of software.

Now, I write and build: long-form essays, research prototypes, and small tools that make research easier to reproduce.
